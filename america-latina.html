Maintenance América Latina Bookstore Backoffice

The maintenance of the América Latina Bookstore back office stands out from my other responsibilities due to the comprehensive overhaul I have carried out on the entire system. This large bookstore in Uruguay is not only a major retailer but also an importer of books and a supplier to several other bookstores.

For the past two years, I have maintained the entire system, which includes the back office, book stock system, payment systems, customer-facing front end, and databases. My work encompasses all fronts, developed using Angular and Express. Additionally, the bookstore's systems are integrated with a book stock management system called BIT.

One of my proudest achievements is the complete reformation of the bookstore's website, ensuring it operates efficiently and meets modern standards. This year, we also made a significant advancement by making the bookstore's API available to other book-selling stores. This allows our partners to access the same information as their supplier, streamlining the process and improving overall efficiency.

Being a crucial pillar for this esteemed company brings me great pride, as my efforts directly contribute to its success and reputation in the market.

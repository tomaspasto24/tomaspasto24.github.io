Promoción Supergás (ANCAP from MATRIZ), May 2024

Website designed for overseeing the monthly supergas draw. Tailored Frontend development implemented with React and microservices utilizing NestJS for the draw and registration processes of the precincts. The system uses the Unique System of Geographic Addresses of Uruguay (IDE) to provide options for locations within Uruguay that the user has the possibility of choosing.

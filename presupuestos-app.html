Presupuestos App (Ismael Perdomo, Publicidad Digital), July 2022

PresupuestosApp is a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) e-commerce platform developed using React Native for the mobile app, NextJS for the web application, and Strapi with Express for the backend. The platform is designed specifically for service providers in Uruguay, allowing them to showcase their profiles effectively. It shares similarities with major e-commerce platforms in Latin America, but with a distinct emphasis on service providers.
